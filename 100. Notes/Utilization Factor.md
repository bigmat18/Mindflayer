# Utilization Factor

The utilization factor of a process Q is defined as $\rho_{Q} = T_{id-Q}/T_{A-Q}$ Two cases
- **Bottleneck**: $\rho > 1$
- **Non-bottleneck**: $\rho \leq 1$
If Utilization Factor is too less of 1 is bad because there is a wasted resourced.
